💡 Storage, Shelf Life & Pro Tips

Storage

  • Airtight glass jars, kept dark and cool (pantry shelf, not fridge).
  • Never store with strong-smelling foods — tea absorbs odors aggressively.
  • Label each tin: fusion name, blend date, batch number.

Shelf Life

Blend TypeFresh Window
Rooibos, hibiscus, herbal fusions12–18 months
Black tea fusions (Silk Route)12 months
Oolong & green fusions (Monsoon Bloom, Obsidian Mate)6–9 months
Saffron (separate)2 yrs sealed / 6 mo opened

Pro Tips

  1. Blend small batches weekly — freshness shows in the cup.
  2. Always weigh, never eyeball — the flavor chart holds only if ratios are consistent.
  3. Keep a 10 g "reference" from your best batch and taste-test against it.
  4. Pre-portion event tins into 2.5 g single-serve sachets for pop-ups.
  5. Saffron and cacao nibs go in-cup, not in-tin — they lose aroma in blends over time.
  6. Photograph every new batch for @cupsandcultures.co — the ritual is the brand.
  7. Keep a brewing log: water hardness, temperature, steep time, guest reactions.
