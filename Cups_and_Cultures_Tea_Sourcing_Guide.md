## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Practice the Moroccan pour** with water first - it takes skill!
2. **Soak clay kulhads** in water for 30 minutes before first use
3. **Buy whole spices** for chai - much better flavor than pre-ground
4. **Clear glass cups** showcase the beautiful colors of herbal infusions
5. **Consider buying extra** of the first event teas - you'll need them for multiple tastings
